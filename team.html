Meet the Team

We're a small team of passionate people, supported by an extended network and our amazing community of hitchhikers.

The Team

Adam Renak

Co-founder & Product Lead

Passionate about sustainable travel and building communities. Adam believes hitchhiking is the ultimate form of human connection and wants to make it accessible to everyone.

Dan Smith

Co-founder & Tech Lead

Tech enthusiast with a love for adventure. Dan combines his engineering background with his passion for travel to build the technology that makes hitchhiking safer and more accessible.

Supported by

Mara Alasia

User Experience

Mahpara Nawal

Rachel Renak

User Experience

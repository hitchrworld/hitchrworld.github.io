This isn't just about getting from A to B. It's about communication, trust, teamwork, and stepping outside comfort zones. Participants will need to work together, make quick decisions, and rely on the kindness of strangers – creating bonds and memories that last long after the event ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do events typically last?

Most events run for 4-8 hours, but we can customise the duration to fit your schedule. This includes the pre-event briefing, the hitchhiking challenge itself, and the celebration at the finish point.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking 6-12 weeks in advance to ensure availability and allow time for proper planning. However, we can accommodate shorter timelines for smaller groups.

What happens in bad weather?

Safety comes first. We monitor weather closely and have contingency plans in place. If conditions are unsafe, we can reschedule at no additional cost or adapt the event route.

Do participants need special equipment?

No special equipment required! We provide everything participants need, including safety materials, tracking devices, and branded materials. We'll send a simple packing list (comfortable shoes, weather-appropriate clothing).

Where can events take place?

We run events across the UK and Europe. Popular routes include countryside challenges, coastal adventures, and city-to-city experiences. We'll work with you to choose the perfect location.

What if someone can't or doesn't want to participate?

Participation is always voluntary. We can arrange alternative roles (e.g., tracking dashboard monitor, finish line coordinator) or simply excuse individuals – no judgment, no pressure.

What's included in the price?

All packages include route planning, safety briefings, real-time tracking, event support team, insurance, and emergency support. Premium and Enterprise packages include additional features like branding, photography, and extended workshops.

How do you ensure participant safety?

We use a multi-layered approach: comprehensive pre-event training, carefully scouted routes, real-time GPS tracking, 24/7 support team, full insurance coverage, and clear emergency protocols. Safety is our #1 priority.

Will there be winners or competition?

That's up to you! We can structure events as friendly competitions (first to arrive, most creative routes, best stories) or purely collaborative experiences. We're flexible based on your team culture.

Saying Goodbye to Hitchr

We're sorry to hear you're considering leaving Hitchr! Before you go, we want to make sure you understand that deleting your account is a permanent action. Your hitches and any other account data will be unrecoverable.

Delete your account

Send an email to farewell@hitchr.world. In the email body, please include the email address associated with the account you wish to delete.

Important Information

  • Deleting your account can take up to 7 business days to complete.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once your account deletion is finalized.

We'd love you to stay for the journey, what can we do?

If you're having any issues, or would like to discuss alternative options before deleting your account, please reach out to our support team at support@hitchr.world. We understand that sometimes it's time to move on, thanks for coming along for the ride.

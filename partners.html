Meet Our Partners

Organisations that share our values and are helping us redefine hitchhiking for the modern traveller.

Helpbnk (and Simon Squibb)

Simon and the Helpbnk team have helped us kickstart our dream. They've built an amazing community of fellow dreamers who are working together to build a better world – and they've been cheering us on since day one.

Arbor

We're working with Arbor to better understand our community beyond the people we speak to directly. Their insights are helping us see who the Hitchr audience really is, and how we can reach more of them in the most effective way.

An Honest Day's Work

Jacob is helping us transform Hitchr from just an idea and a colour palette into a story and a movement. Through thoughtful design and creative direction, he's helped shape how we present ourselves to the world.

Special Thanks

We're grateful to these communities and platforms that help us do what we do.

Hitchmap

We use Hitchmap data to show people good places to get picked up from within Hitchr. We're very grateful to the Hitchmap team and community for supporting our project with their open data and collaborative spirit.

Can you help us build a more connected world?

We work with partners who believe, like we do, that the world is better when people meet in real life.

Hitchr is about more than getting from A to B – it's about trust, spontaneity and human connection. We work with brands, communities and individuals who share those values and want to support a more open, sustainable way to travel.

Whatever you can offer, we'd love to hear from you.

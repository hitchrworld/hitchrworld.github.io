The Lightbulb Moment

That was our lightbulb moment. We knew we had something worth building ‚Äì so we set off to speak with other hitchhikers, to learn what they loved, what they feared, and what they needed. Their stories shaped the foundations of what Hitchr is today.

Why Hitchhiking?

Because the world is full of empty car seats, and people are better than we've been led to believe.

Efficient & Low-Impact

Hitchhiking is one of the most efficient, low-impact ways to get around. Every shared ride reduces emissions and makes the world a little greener.

Surprisingly Joyful

There's something magical about the connections you make on the road. Strangers become friends, and journeys become adventures.

Human Connection

In a world of screens and isolation, hitchhiking brings people together. Real conversations, real stories, real human moments.

But over time, it's been pushed to the fringes ‚Äì seen as risky, outdated, or just plain odd.

We think it deserves better.

What We're Building

Hitchr helps you track your hitchhiking trips and share your adventures with friends, family and other travellers.

We're not a taxi app, and we're not rideshare. We don't do money.

We're here to make hitchhiking safer, smarter and more accessible ‚Äì by giving people the tools, information and community support they need to travel with confidence.
